ABOUT CRAZY CATZ

THE CRAZY CATZ NFT COLLECTION IS THE NEXT GENERATION OF EXPERIMENTAL WEB3 COLLECTABLES. WE ARE ON A MISSION TO BRIDGE THE DIGITAL WITH THE PHYSICAL THROUGH PARTNERSHIPS AND COLLABORATIONS WITHIN BOTH CHINESE AND WESTERN CULTURE.

OUR GENESIS COLLECTION IS COMPRISED OF 8,888 INDIVIDUALLY UNIQUE NFTS (DESIGNED BY WILLIAM LINDHORST) AND IS EMBLAZONED WITH OVER 200 TRAITS THAT ARE A REFLECTION OF BOTH WESTERN AND ASIAN CULTURE.

THE IMPETUS FOR THIS PROJECT STEMS FROM WANTING TO CREATE AN NFT COLLECTION THAT FORMS REAL-WORLD PARTNERSHIPS WITH ESTABLISHED COMPANIES AND UP-AND-COMING BRANDS, BOTH IN WEB3 AND AT LARGE.

OUR
MISSION

To be normal is to conform to standards. To be normal is to not strive for greatness and perfection. To be normal is to be forgotten, lost among the masses.
We hate normal. We despise normal. Because normal is not remembered. That’s why we’re crazy. All the greats in history were far from normal. They despised it too. This drove them to do great things. This is our aim.
We are Crazy Catz. We scoff at the rules, and set our sights on goals far beyond what society expects. To be crazy is to fight mediocrity. We are not reckless, as we control our crazy. This is our secret weapon.
Crazy, when controlled and used properly, is our greatest asset. It pushes us further and closer to our goals, far past what we once thought was possible.
If you’re reading this, there is a good chance you are a little crazy yourself. Lean in and watch the magic happen. Welcome to Crazy Catz.

ROADMAP 2.0

The best ideas are usually unplanned. The initial Crazy Catz Roadmap covers an array of fields related to web3 and physical-world integration, all aimed at achieving one common goal: build Crazy Catz, its I.P., and community to be the strongest in the sphere of NFTs and web3. We want to be the Hello Kitty of NFTs. Holders will help guide and shape our future in real-time, together with the founding team.
